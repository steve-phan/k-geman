# Privacy Policy for K-Geman App

**Effective Date:** February 11, 2026

## Data Collection

K-Geman is a German grammar learning application that **collects no personal data** from its users.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Camera or photo access
- Contact information
- Device identifiers
- Analytics or usage data

## Content Access

The app downloads German grammar content (verbs, articles, and clause examples) from Firebase Firestore for educational purposes. This content is:
- Publicly accessible
- Does not require user authentication
- Cached locally on your device for offline use

## Internet Permission

The app requires internet permission solely to:
- Download grammar content from Firebase Firestore
- Check for content updates (weekly)

No personal data is transmitted over the network.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase Firestore only for content delivery. No user data is sent to Firebase or any third-party service.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document.
